About me

My story began in my college dorm room. I was yearning change and adventure after living in the same small town for 17 years. During college, I began working long hours and saving every dime. After a year out of college, I've visited over ten countries.

Why do you love to travel?

Traveling is an adventure of the unknown. When you go to another location, you are confronted with all kind of unexpected challenges.

That's why I enjoy traveling so much: it challenges me to broaden my horizons and reassess my assumptions. opportunity to learn from those whose life experiences differ from mine As a result, patience, wisdom, perspective, and appreciation have grown in me.

Why did you start blogging

I started TinyWanderlust to share my travel adventures and experiences with you in the hopes of igniting the wanderlust in all of us.
